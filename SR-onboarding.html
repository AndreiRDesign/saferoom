Before you begin

SafeRoom is a personal space to explore and celebrate your cultural identity. You will be asked to share memories, stories, and reflections that are meaningful to you.

We want you to feel safe doing so. Here is what you should know:

  • Your data never leaves your device. Everything you write is stored locally in your browser — not on any server.
  • We do not collect, transmit, or store your entries. No one at SafeRoom can read what you write.
  • Your stories are yours. We will never use your personal information for research, marketing, or any other purpose.
  • Clearing your browser data will erase your SafeRoom. There is no cloud backup.

This project was created as part of a design thesis. It is not a commercial product and does not operate a backend or database of any kind.
